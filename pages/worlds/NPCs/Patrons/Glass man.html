Glass man

The glass man is a being, perhaps an old god, who is the patron of  Juno Whitfoot. According to Seko Shura, he's also able to shape-shift and isn't a person that should be easily trusted. Little else is known about this figure, although he almost certainly has ill intentions. 

Throughout the game the glass man has entrusted Juno with a variety of boons, including the ability to teleport short distances, limited spell-casting, and an orb which can transform into any instrument. He appears to reside in a dimension made up entirely of glass shards, which Juno is able to visit at will by using his ‘Gift of the void’ on any doorway.
