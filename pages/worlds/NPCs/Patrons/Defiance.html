Defiance

The following is from the notes of Thomas Vic:

"Defiance is an other worldly entity that serves as the Shadow Queen’s top lieutenant. They have yet to appear to our intrepid heroes as a group but has a long standing alliance with Thomas , despite the two’s opposing aims. Defiance’s grand plan is unclear but from their offers it is clear they hates tyrants and would love more than to see Thomas fall to their ideology of hate and rage.

Defiance has many children who take the form of black dragons in their lair atop the Black Mountain. Defiance themselves takes the form of a vorpal pit of darkness."
