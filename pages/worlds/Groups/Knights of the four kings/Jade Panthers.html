Jade Panthers

The Jade Panthers are one of the orders of the Knights of the four kings, specifically specializing in agile combat. They're well known for using stealth tactics rather than brute force.

“The Jade Panthers are dexterous predators, they believe in the kill and only the kill when it comes to fighting, often contrasting the Iron Bears, the panthers tend to prefer all manner of sneak attacks to take down their prey and avoid upfront confrontation when safer and more effective methods present themselves.”
