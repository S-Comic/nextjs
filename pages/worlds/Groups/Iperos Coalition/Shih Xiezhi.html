Shih Xiezhi

Shih Xiezhi is the first mate of the Iperos Coalition and princess of the Qian Kingdoms. She can be identified by her unique fashion sense, often wearing a stylish and easily identifiable red dress. Shih has the ability to ‘phase’ for lack of a better word: she has a knack for moving incredibly quickly and avoiding direct sight, as demonstrated by her observation of the party as they explored Sereni.

The following is from the notes of Thomas Vic:

"The Red Dressed Ghost has remained a mostly suspicious but ineffable figure to our intrepid heroes. They suspect she had been tailing them since they entered Sereni but at dinner at the Bousant’s she denied having left the compound".
